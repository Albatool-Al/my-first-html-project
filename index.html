Introduction to Urban Gardening

Urban gardening is the practice of growing plants in an urban environment. It has gained popularity as people seek to reconnect with nature, grow their own food, and beautify their living spaces.

Even with limited space, you can create a thriving garden using containers, vertical spaces, and creative solutions.

Why Start an Urban Garden?

  • Fresh produce: Grow your own organic vegetables and herbs
  • Stress relief: Gardening is a proven way to reduce stress
  • Environmental impact: Helps reduce your carbon footprint
  • Community building: Connect with neighbors through shared gardens

Getting Started

1. Assess Your Space

Look at your balcony, windowsills, or even walls that get sunlight.

2. Choose Your Plants

Start with easy-to-grow plants like herbs, lettuce, or cherry tomatoes.

3. Gather Supplies

You'll need containers, potting soil, seeds or seedlings, and basic tools.
